Youth Speak Forum

48-Hour Hackathon for SDGs

Led by AIESEC Thyna, step into a dynamic 48-hour hackathon where young innovators collaborate to solve challenges tied to three Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). This fast-paced event blends creativity, technology, and social impact, giving participants the tools to design scalable solutions. Be part of this transformative experience and help shape a better future—one idea at a time!

About The Event

The Youth Speak Forum is a platform for young leaders to voice their opinions and take action on global issues through innovative projects.

48-Hour Hackathon

Intensive 2-day event where participants collaborate to develop solutions for pressing African challenges.

Focus on SDGs

Projects will address three key Sustainable Development Goals selected for Africa's development.

Youth Empowerment

Platform for young African leaders to develop skills, network, and create impactful projects.
